CSS OVERFLOW

CSS Overflow-Visible

The overflow property specifies whether to clip the content or to add scrollbars when the content of an element is too big to fit in the specified area. The overflow has four types of values,they are visible,hidden,scroll and auto. This all has its own purposes.
