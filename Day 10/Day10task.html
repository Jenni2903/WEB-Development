Types of Transport Vehicles

AEROPLANE

An aeroplane, also known as an airplane in American English, is a fixed-wing aircraft propelled by thrust. It's a vehicle designed for air travel with wings and one or more engines.Airplanes are used for various purposes, including transportation, recreation, military, and research. They are generally faster than other forms of transport like cars, trains, or boats, making them ideal for long distances.

TRAIN

Rail transport in India consists of primarily of passenger and freight shipments along an integrated rail network. Indian Railways (IR), a statutory body under the ownership of the Ministry of Railways of the Government of India, operates India's national railway system.

SHIP

Ship, any large floating vessel capable of crossing open waters, as opposed to a boat, which is generally a smaller craft. The term formerly was applied to sailing vessels having three or more masts; in modern times it usually denotes a vessel of more than 500 tons of displacement.

CAR

"Car" generally refers to a four-wheeled motor vehicle used for passenger transportation, similar to how it's used worldwide. These vehicles typically seat one to eight people and are primarily used for travel on roads, distinguishing them from cargo-carrying vehicles or those with different numbers of wheels.
